Design patterns are common approaches to solving similar problems. The [1995 book Design Patterns, by the so-called Gang of Four (GoF)](http://amzn.to/1GYRo2O), describes 23 patterns. Quickly learn about several patterns and how to learn new patters in this [Design Patterns Overview course on Pluralsight](https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/design-patterns-overview). [Pluralsight's design patterns library](http://bit.ly/DesignPatternsLibrary) includes 32 different patterns, including most of the original GoF patterns. Some design patterns tend to cause more problems than they solve, and are thus commonly referred to as [antipatterns](/docs/antipatterns/antipatterns-overview/).
